Locusts

Ingredients:

  • 25 locusts (or more, whichever you fancy)
  • 10 cups of vegetable stock
  • Tumeric (won't need much)
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Pour vegetable stock into pot and bring to a boil
  2. Add the tumeric to the vegetable stock
  3. Once boiling, place locusts into pot for 3 minutes
  4. Remove and drain locusts from the pot. Leave to cool for 5 minutes
  5. Remove wings and legs
  6. Season your flower with salt and black pepper then place locusts in flour
  7. Place flour coated locust back into pot for about 5 minutes
  8. ENJOY!
